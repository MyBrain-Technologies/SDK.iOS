################# calibrationRecordings5s ########################
calibrationRecordings5s contains the EEG values of the calibration.
This is an array of 2 rows (2 channels) and 250x5 columns (EEG values).

################## calibrationRecordingsQuality_BOTHBad ########################
calibrationRecordingsQuality_BOTHBad contains the qualities of the EEG packets
of 1s during calibration. This is an array of 2 rows (2 channels) and 5
columns.
